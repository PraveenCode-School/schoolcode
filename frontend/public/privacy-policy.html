Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Welcome to C2C (Connect to Campus). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Important: By using the C2C application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us when using the app, including but not limited to:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, user ID
  • Student Information: Student name, roll number, class, section, academic year, date of birth
  • Staff Information: Employee ID, designation, department, contact details
  • Parent Information: Parent/guardian name, contact details, relationship to student
  • School Information: School name, school code, academic details

1.2 Academic and Administrative Data

  • Attendance Records: Daily attendance, leave applications, attendance history
  • Fee Information: Fee structure, payment history, dues, receipts
  • Academic Records: Marks, grades, certificates, exam results
  • Hostel Data: Room allocation, mess bills, hostel dues
  • Transport Data: Bus routes, fleet tracking, student pickup/drop locations

1.3 Device and Usage Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Push Notification Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for sending notifications
  • Location Data: GPS location for live fleet tracking (only when using transport features)
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, features accessed, error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide core app functionality including attendance tracking, fee management, marks entry, certificate generation, and hostel management
  • Communication: To send important notifications about attendance, fees, announcements, and academic updates
  • Authentication: To verify user identity and maintain secure access to the application
  • Live Fleet Tracking: To provide real-time bus location tracking for student safety
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the app and improve our services
  • Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activities

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Within School Community

Your information may be shared within your school's authorized users:

  • School Administrators: Have access to all student, staff, and academic data
  • Teachers: Can view student attendance, marks, and academic records for their classes
  • Parents: Can view their own child's attendance, fees, marks, and certificates
  • Students: Can view their own academic records, attendance, and fee details

3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the app:

  • Cloud Hosting: Amazon Web Services (AWS) for hosting our servers and databases
  • Push Notifications: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) for sending notifications
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve performance

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure databases with access controls
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication and secure login mechanisms
  • Regular Backups: Automatic data backups to prevent data loss
  • Access Control: Role-based access to ensure users only see authorized information
Note: While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations (academic records retention requirements)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Maintain historical academic records for certificate verification

Academic records (marks, certificates, attendance history) may be retained indefinitely for official purposes even after account deletion.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information stored in the app
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Location Services: Disable GPS tracking in your device settings (may affect fleet tracking features)

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for use by educational institutions and may be used by students of various ages. We collect and process student information only with the consent and authorization of the school administration and parents/guardians.

Parents/guardians have the right to review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's personal information by contacting their school administrator.

8. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications about:

  • Daily attendance updates
  • Fee payment reminders and receipts
  • Academic announcements
  • Exam schedules and results
  • Emergency alerts

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings. This will not affect your ability to use the app.

9. Location Services

We collect GPS location data only when you use the Live Fleet Tracking feature. This data is used exclusively to:

  • Display real-time bus locations on the map
  • Help parents track their child's school bus
  • Improve student safety during transportation

Location tracking can be disabled in your device settings, but this will prevent access to the fleet tracking feature.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Sending a push notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
