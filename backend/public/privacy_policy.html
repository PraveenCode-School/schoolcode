Privacy Policy for Connect to Campus

Last updated: February 03, 2026

Connect to Campus ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed by Connect to Campus.

Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our educational services, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, student/staff ID, and contact details provided by your institution.
  • Academic Data: Attendance records, marks, library history, and fee details.
  • Device Information: We may collect device IDs for push notifications and account security.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide academic and administrative services.
  • Send notifications regarding fees, exams, and holidays.
  • Secure your account and prevent unauthorized access.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
